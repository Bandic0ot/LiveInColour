About Me

I was born and raised in Saskatoon, Canada. This means I learned exactly two things growing up. Firstly, summers here are beautiful. They're a time for kayaking, playing soccer, and doing whatever activities that may be happening downtown. Secondly, you better have a good imagination come winter. The latter reason is what drove my imagination and curiosity as a child. A combination which steered me in the direction of video-games, then to tinkering with electronics, and then eventually, programming. It's what I've grown up to love and what I'm now passionate about.

I work hard and I like to have fun and make friends along the way. I strive to improve myself at every given opportunity and will do my best to help you create the software solution you need.

I look forward to working with you!

*This site serves as a project on it's own. Built from the ground up and then redone in Bootstrap, it is a showcase for all my other projects and will change over time to reflect that. Until then, enjoy some photos I took from around the world!
