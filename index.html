About Me

Born and raised in Saskatoon, SK, my whole life I grew up playing video-games. I was always curious as a kid asking "How does this work?" or "How can I make this better?". This curiosity, something I carry with myself to this day, is what drives me. It's what set me down the path of becoming a software developer.

I work hard and I like to have fun and make friends along the way. I strive to improve myself in every way and will strive to help you make the best product possible.

I look forward to working with you!
